Memories, Regrets, Apologies … and Forgiveness

Memories, Regrets, Apologies … and Forgiveness is the first record of his solo project, an interesting, obscure and minimalist EP that explores the most hidden memories of the musician. The whole project has been developed by himself, from the recording and editing to the mastering. You can download it for free in David Orchards Bandcamp or even pay for it whatever you want. Thank you for your support!

Mowgli Kill Pop

In October 2015 during his stay in London, David meets Manu Lahuerta, leader of the noise-pop band Mowgli Kill Pop. He decided to join Manu playing the bass. After a few months, David produced and edited their six tracks LP called “Pop (is gospel music)”.
